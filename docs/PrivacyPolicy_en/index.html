2. General Information

Donkey Bridge is a completely offline password generation app.
We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data – neither on your device nor on servers.

  • No cloud synchronization.

All calculations take place exclusively on your local device (smartphone, tablet, etc.).
Your inputs (e.g., keywords or quotes) are processed only temporarily in RAM (memory) and deleted immediately after generation. There is no permanent storage of your inputs anywhere within the app.
