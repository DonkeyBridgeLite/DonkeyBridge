Donkey Bridge Lite – Quick Guide

What is Donkey Bridge Lite?

Donkey Bridge Lite generates secure and unique passwords for every account — completely locally, without storage and without any cloud.
Example: You only keep one single Crypto-Line in your head and get exactly the same strong password from every stored number sequence.

Crypto-Line (the ONLY thing you have to remember!)

You really only need to remember this one Crypto-Line — nothing else!
The Crypto-Line must have at least 8 characters for security reasons, but can be as long as you like — even a full sentence, song lyric or movie quote.

Examples: „Mamba17_” or „Think it. Recall it. Donkey Bridge Lite” or „Think it. Recall it. Donkey Bridge Pro“ — that’s all you ever have to memorize.

Account notation is always a number sequence

The notation is always a number sequence from 1 to 8 or more, each number appearing exactly once and separated by commas.
You simply copy this number sequence from your PDF or e-mail and paste it into the notation field (R-Tool).
Example: In the PDF it says: 1, 3, 4, 8, 2, 7, 5, 6 – Gmail private.

How to retrieve a password (R-Tool):

  1. Open your e-mail or PDF.
  2. Copy the corresponding number sequence (e.g. 1,3,4,8,2,7,5,6).
  3. Open Donkey Bridge Lite → R-Tool.
  4. Paste the number sequence into the notation field.
  5. Type your complete Crypto-Line (short or long).
  6. Press “Reconstruct”.
  7. The finished password appears below → can be copied and used for login.

Example: You paste 1,3,4,8...,28, type „Houston, we have no problem!“ → your password appears instantly.

Security

  • 100 % local on your device
  • No password is ever stored
  • No internet required

Example: The longer your Crypto-Line, the more secure each password becomes — yet you still only have to know this one line.

Summary — what you really need to know

Remember exactly ONE thing: your Crypto-Line — at least 8 characters, as long as you like (short code or full mnemonic from song/movie/book).
All number sequences are safely stored in your PDF or e-mail and simply copied.
Example: You can use „Mamba17_“ or „Think it. Recall it. Donkey Bridge Lite“ — both work perfectly.

How many passwords are possible

With just one single Crypto-Line you can generate 8×8 = 64 different, absolutely secure passwords — more than enough for a normal person’s entire life.

The best part

With Donkey Bridge Lite you only ever need one single password (your Crypto-Line) in your head for the rest of your life — Donkey Bridge Lite automatically creates all other strong passwords for you.
